OpenBeavs is a shared AI workspace built for Oregon State University students and faculty. Sign in once with your OSU Microsoft account and immediately access a growing registry of specialized AI agents — from an OSU knowledge-base assistant to creative writing coaches and real-time weather experts. Agents can even collaborate with each other to give you richer, multi-step answers.

## How it works

OpenBeavs connects a SvelteKit frontend to a FastAPI backend and a collection of standalone AI agent servers. Every agent — whether built by the OpenBeavs team or a third-party developer — communicates using the **A2A protocol**: a simple JSON-RPC 2.0 standard with a `/.well-known/agent.json` discovery card.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in with your OSU account">
    Use your existing OSU Microsoft credentials via SSO — no new passwords or accounts required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse the agent registry">
    Explore agents contributed by OSU teams. Each agent card shows its capabilities, skills, and version.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install and chat">
    Add an agent to your workspace with one click, then select it from the chat model dropdown and start asking questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your own (optional)">
    Any HTTP service that serves a `/.well-known/agent.json` card can join the registry. See the [agent development guide](/agents/building-agents) to get started.
  </Step>
</Steps>
